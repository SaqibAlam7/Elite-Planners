About Elite Planners

Elite Planners is a boutique event planning studio dedicated to creating meaningful, stylish and well‑organized experiences for clients who value quality and detail.

Every event is treated like a one‑of‑a‑kind project with its own story, mood, and purpose. From the first consultation to the final guest departure, Elite Planners ensures every element works together beautifully.

The focus is on clear communication, transparent planning, and calm, professional coordination so you feel supported at every step.

  • •Concept & theme development
  • •Venue scouting and layout planning
  • •Vendor curation and negotiations
  • •Detailed timelines & checklists
  • •On‑site supervision and guest flow management
Whether you are planning a wedding in Aligarh or a destination event elsewhere, Elite Planners ensures your celebration feels effortless, elegant, and truly yours.
